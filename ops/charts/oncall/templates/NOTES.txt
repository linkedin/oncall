## For internal access

The Oncall service can be accessed via port {{ .Values.port.external }} on the following DNS names from within your cluster:

    `oncall` or `oncall.{{ .Release.Namespace }}.svc.cluster.local`


## For external access

Oncall service is configured to use ClusterIP type, you can use the following
command to get the external port for the service:

    kubectl describe services oncall
